How to post a video

We currently only support Youtube videos to be shared on E-Pal. To attach videos to your posts, simply press the “Insert Video” button when you post, and paste the link of the video in the box provided.

Find the video you wish to share and open it. There is a “Share” button to the bottom right of the video. Press the button to find the link you could use on E-Pal. Copy and paste the link in the prompt we provide.
